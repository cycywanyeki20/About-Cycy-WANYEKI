NACOSTI stands for the National Commission for Science, Technology and Innovation. It is a Kenyan state corporation responsible for regulating and assuring quality in the research, science, technology, and innovation sector, as well as advising the government on related matters. The commission promotes, regulates, coordinates, and advises on all research and innovation activities in the country.
